 An exploration of digital audio, the {\ttfamily wav} audio format and wavetable synthesis disguised as a non real-\/time synth using only standard C++17

\item polyphonic capabilities (summing {\ttfamily Float\+Data} instances)
\item Band limited wavetables (using the additive engine + brushing up on Fourier series)

\item VCA envelopes
\item maybe write some algorithmic reverb to make it sound good {\itshape good}
\item a sequencer maybe ??
